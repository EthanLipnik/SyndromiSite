# Syndromi

An amazing app to watch your favorite videos, with a beautiful design, for iOS.

### What can Syndromi do?

* View your subscriptions.
* View your subscriptions feed.
* Watch and share videos.
* Picture in Picture.

### Plans

* Add comments.
* Add recommendations.
* Manage subscriptions.
